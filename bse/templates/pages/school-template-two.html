Bodwell High School

Bodwell High School is a private, co-educational boarding school offering grades 8 to 12, and university preparation. Bodwell’s campus is located in the city of North Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. Recognized as a leader among Canadian boarding schools, our school’s diverse student population of both local and international students makes it a rich and exciting place to learn, study, play, and build life-long memories and friendships. Bodwell is registered with the British Columbia Ministry of Education. All graduates who successfully complete their Grade 12 academic program with Bodwell receive the Dogwood Diploma, a globally recognized certificate of graduation, issued by the province of British Columbia. Most of our students go on to seek post-secondary education in colleges or universities across the globe even after Bodwell, many with academic scholarships.

Founded in September 1991, in Vancouver, BC, Canada. Moved in 2003 to her new facilities located in Noth Vancouver, with a full-size gym, an indoor swimming pool, and full-service cafeteria, accommdaing 400 students and 150 boarders. Celebrated its 20th anniversary in 2011 by undertaking and completing an expansion project with enlarged cafeteria, additional classrooms and dormitories.

The school’s community grew to 450 students, 100 staff, and 4,500 alumni from over 60 countries. More expansion project took place between 2015 and 2016, with the addition of a new, multi-floor dormitory, housing over 500 boarding students atop an expanded library and second gymnasium, wirth more classrooms and a state-of-the-art multimedia auditorium.

Bodwell uses a flexible, balanced approach to teaching, allowing students to independently explore a topic of study or be guided through it. This allows students to find their own unique style and pace for learning. Spupport is always available in the classroom, study groups, and individual tutoring. Bodwell’s University and Career Guidance Team developed strong relationships with post-secondary institutions in Canada, U.S.A. and other countries, secured entrance scholarship opportunities and enrollment in top universities through direct entry and pathway programs in Arts, Humanities, Social Sciences, Sciences, Life Sciences, Engineering, Computer Science, Business Admin/Commerce/Management, Fine Arts, Communications and Film.

Schooling At Bodwell High School

Boarding Facilities

Boarding capacity able to hout about 500 students. Boys and girls live in separate halls on different floors and wings. Each hall is a small neighbourhood run by an advisor, ...who acts as a boarding parent, looking after the needs of each student. Each hall has an office for private conversations and mentorship from staff.

All halls feature a student lounge with a kitchenette, along with common bathrooms, laundry machines, and storage All bedrooms have windows for natural light and most have breathtaking views of either the waterfront or the mountains. Bedrooms vary in design and number of roommates, with most ranging from 2 to 4 boarders. Each student has a bed, desk and wardrobe for their belongings.

Academic Programmes

Two terms (5 courses/term):
Fall (Sept. – Feb.), Spring (Feb. – June). Regular Academic Program (RAP), Academic & English ...Preparation (AEP), University Preparation. Programs are in compliance with the British Columbia Ministry of Education requirement. Successful students receive the Dogwood Diploma (high school diploma issued by the BC Ministry of Education).

Counselling:
Bodwell staff include multilingual counsellors who advise students on academics, post-secondary planning, ...health, and personal well-being, as well as work on emotional / relational problem solving skills. They provide emotional support to young minds finding their way to adulthood.Students and parents can comfortably talk with the counsellors in their preferred languages.
